About

Celebal Technologies is an Indian technology company that specializes in the development of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) solutions. The company was founded in 2017 by a group of technology experts with the goal of making AI accessible to businesses of all sizes. Celebal Technologies provides a range of AI and ML services, including natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, and data engineering. The company's solutions are designed to help businesses automate processes, improve decision-making, and enhance customer experiences.
